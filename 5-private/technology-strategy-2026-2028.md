# Texco Technology Strategy (2026--2028)

> A living document that evolves every week. This is not just
> documentation---it's the blueprint for the future Technology function
> at Texco.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Vision

**Question:** If we do technology properly, what does Texco look like in
two years?

## Example Vision

-   Every repetitive task is automated.
-   Every important metric is visible.
-   Every system is documented.
-   AI is embedded into every department.
-   Technology becomes a profit centre instead of a cost centre.

### Success Metrics

-   Time saved
-   Cost savings
-   Employee satisfaction
-   Faster decision making
-   Reduced business risk

# 11. Technology Principles

Examples:

-   Automate before hiring.
-   Buy before building unless software creates strategic advantage.
-   Every project must deliver measurable business value.
-   Documentation is part of the work.
-   Security is never optional.
-   AI augments people rather than replacing expertise.
-   Simplicity beats complexity.
-   Measure everything.

# Long-Term Goal

The objective of this document is to build the foundation for a scalable
Technology organisation.

This document should naturally demonstrate:
-   Where the company started.
-   What has been delivered.
-   The measurable business value created.
-   The roadmap ahead.
-   The team required to continue growing.

When the business reaches that point, the need for technology leadership
becomes obvious because the function already exists.
