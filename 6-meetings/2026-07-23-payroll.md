## Summary
Walked the end-to-end payroll tool: **timesheet entry → review → reconcile → salary → pipeline/file**.
It ingests emailed timesheet PDFs, extracts/splits them, posts ("ingests") into JobPac, and reconciles
against the pre-pro report. Now live; weekly still run manually, to run **in parallel** next week. Most
of the meeting was the payroll lead specifying checks and rules to build in.

## Topics discussed
- Timesheet ingest from email PDFs (AI extraction, prompt-tunable); missing-timesheet summary.
- Review screen: editable vs locked fields (orange = editable, red line = overwritten); bottom section to be fully automated.
- Reconcile/post to JobPac (reversible); validate against pre-pro and against the manual run.
- Allowances all sourced from the **transaction definition** (group code); rates from the **payroll rates table** (effective-dated).
- Salary run review + checks; approval → ABA → "marked as approved".
- Pay-run numbering, RDOs (both states), access/permissions (separate from JobPac).
- **Institutional-knowledge risk:** payroll rules live largely in one head → [[handover]].

## Decisions (with reasoning)
- **RDO accrual = 10% of OT/OTE hours; RDO paid at 7.2h (not 8)** — confirmed against the award.
- **No allowances on RDOs; demo replaces site allowance; asbestos on top; productivity = flat $4.**
- **All allowances from the transaction definition; all rates from the payroll rates table** (shown rates are validation-only) — single source of truth.
- **Don't auto-generate pay-run numbers** — off-cycle payments + reversals break the sequence; confirm the number at approval instead.
- **Allowances & deductions stay manual for the interim.**

## Decisions & action items (summary)

**Decisions**
- RDO accrual = 10% of OT hours (OTE only: normal time, sick leave, training days, leave). RDO paid at 7.2 hours (not 8) — confirmed.
- Demo allowance replaces site allowance; asbestos allowance is paid on top. No allowances at all on RDOs.
- Productivity allowance is a flat $4 (always paid). Site allowance is contract-sum based (small/medium/large) via the EBA table, with an override for combined contract sums (e.g. China Hall).
- All allowances must come from the **transaction definition** (linked to the employee's group code), all rates from the **payroll rates table** (effective-dated). The rates shown are for validation only.
- Bottom section of the review screen to be fully locked/automated (except adjustments); overwritten fields flagged with a red line.
- Pay-run numbering: don't auto-generate — surface a confirmation of the pay-run number at approval.
- Allowances & deductions stay manual for the interim.
